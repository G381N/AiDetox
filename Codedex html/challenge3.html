The Winger Wore Armani

When the soccer sensation Luis Figo married his sweetheart, Helen Svedin, last year, the couple
walked down the aisle in made-to-measure outfits created by his friend, the designer Giorgio Armani. Figo -- the mercurial Real Madrid winger, who was voted the world's best player of 2001 by the sport's governing body -- had met Armani the previous January, when he attended the designer's runway presentation in Milan.
